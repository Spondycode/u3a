Welcome to the U3A Costa Brava

The U3A is an organisation made up of independent groups run by the members themselves. Being a member of the U3A Costa Brava is a great way to connect with like-minded people living or visiting this stunning area. It is an ideal way to make new friends, build up connections and enjoy the best quality of life that the Costa Brava has to offer.

We provide a wide range of physical and learning activities throughout the year including cycling, walking, crafts, Spanish language practice and many interesting local interest visits. Events are organised by members who wish to use some of their leisure time to share skills or to acquire new ones.

Information about our activities and the latest programmes of events can be accessed through the 'Groups' page.

Casual Coffee Mornings

Casual coffee mornings are a chance for old and new members to catch up over a coffee. Please bring a friend or neighbour and one of our committee or group activity leaders can explain what we're all about.
